About

The Weather App is a free online platform designed to provide users with real-time weather information for cities worldwide. Its purpose is to offer users easy access to up-to-date weather data, helping them plan activities, travels, and stay informed about weather conditions in their local area or any global destination. Whether for travelers, residents, or weather enthusiasts, the app's user-friendly interface and comprehensive data make it a valuable resource for weather-related decision-making.
